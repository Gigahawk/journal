# Money BBQ review

Got the 3 meat combo, BBQ pork, crispy pork, BBQ duck.

Crispy pork is probably one of the best I've had in Vancouver texture wise, maybe a little under seasoned.
Duck was also very good, not too dry.
BBQ pork had good texture but could have had a more flavorful sauce.
The broccoli was cold.
Rice was serviceable but not particularly good, also they give you quite a lot of rice.
Everything is served in a slightly too small container so it's hard to get to the rice without first eating a bunch of the meat.

Milk tea was fine, maybe slightly less sweeter than I expected for regular sweetness.
